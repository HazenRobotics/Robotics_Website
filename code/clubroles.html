What Roles are Available

At Hazen Robotics, we divide the various activites we do in smaller tasks that indivdual members or a group of members can work on. For our competitons, we have two main groups working on the robot: Design and Fabrication(The Hardware Team) , and Programming. Additonally, we need money every year to fund our operation, which is why we have a outreach team as well. For more information on specific roles in the team, scroll down.

Roles Information
Project Manager Organizes the Team
Hardware Lead Manages the design and making of the robot
Programming Lead Manages the programming of the robot
Business Lead Manages the finances of the team
Sustainbility Lead Trains new members
Designer Designs part of the robot using math, physics, and CAD(computer assisted drafting)
Fabricators Use various machines and tools to create parts for the robot.
Programmers Program various parts of the robot(mainly in Java). Use FTC Documentation.
