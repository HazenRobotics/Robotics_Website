First Tech Challenege

Hazen Robotics has two Robotics Teams: the Scarabs(Team 8693),and the first year team Multiversal omni kittens and unicorns(TBD). Both teams compete in First Tech Challenege(FTC) Washington. The Scarabs consisentally make it to the State competitons and have improved year after year. Our ultimate goal is to reach Worlds. FTC competions occur in the order of two league meets, interleagues, followed by semifinals, then State, and then for the lucky few, Worlds. At interleagues, we have consisentally won the inspire award, which is given for teams that do the most to contribute the expanding the footprint of robotics and stem in their communites.
