First Tech Challenege

Hazen Robotics has two Robotics Teams: the Scarabs(Team 8693), and the first-year team The Ouroboros(TBD). Both teams compete in the First Tech Challenge(FTC). Washington. The Scarabs consistently make it to the State competitions and have improved year after year. Our ultimate goal is to reach Worlds. FTC competitions occur in the order of two league meets, interleague, followed by semifinals, then State, and then for the lucky few, Worlds. At interleague, we have consistently won the Inspire Award which is given to teams that do the most to contribute to expanding the footprint of robotics and STEM in their communities.
