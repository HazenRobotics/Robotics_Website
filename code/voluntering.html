At Hazen Robotics, we contribute back to our Renton community in various ways through volunteering. We use our expertise in STEM to mentor elementery FLL(First Lego League) and middle school FTC(First Tech Challenge) Teams. Additonally, we are involved in Public Relations(PR) events like career or stems nights, summer camps, holiday events, and more! Robotics members can earn hunderds of hours of volunteering over their high school career just form the events we show up at!
